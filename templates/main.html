Multi-factor-authentication combines an identifier (e.g. your email address) with something you know (a password) and something you have (a token).
Managing multiple accounts and passwords online is much safer and easier with a password manager, but managing tokens across sites is still hard.
Why not use something similar for your tokens?
This app allows you to securely store your tokens in a central location.
Features include:
  • Strong multi-factor authentication including hardware token support FIDO/U2F
  • Federated login with Google
  • Token sharing
  • Open-source!
